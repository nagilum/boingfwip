Boingfwip

API that gives you a JSON "echo" of your request.

How To Use

It's pretty simple. You just perform a request towards https://api.boingfwip.net/ with any HTTP method and it will return a JSON with your request info/headers/etc and payload.

Example

If you do a GET in your browser towards https://api.boingfwip.net/, you should get something like this in return. 

{"http":{"version":"1.1","major":1,"minor":1,"method":"GET"},"connection":{"remoteAddress":"193.90.234.18"},"headers":{"connection":"upgrade","host":"api.boingfwip.net","accept-encoding":"gzip","x-forwarded-proto":"https","upgrade-insecure-requests":"1","user-agent":"Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 10.0; Win64; x64) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/75.0.3770.100 Safari/537.36","dnt":"1","accept":"text/html,application/xhtml+xml,application/xml;q=0.9,image/webp,image/apng,*/*;q=0.8,application/signed-exchange;v=b3","accept-language":"en-GB,en;q=0.9,en-US;q=0.8,nb;q=0.7,sv;q=0.6,de;q=0.5","cookie":"__cfduid=d2f720d3274f5494468c3672fac9ff15f1561620649"},"query":{},"body":{"raw":null}}

What's With the Name?

A phrase used by the character Ted and his band on the popular television series "scrubs". This is a short hand version of "I'm rubber and you're glue" implying someone's insult bounces off of you, creating a "boing" sound and is thrown back through the air making a "fwip" sound. Boing fwip is often accompanied by hand movements implying said action.

person 1: You're a jerkface
person 2: Boing fwip!
jerk face (formerly known as person 1): Oh snap

Source: urbandictionary.com

I'm terrible at naming my projects and went through several rounds in my head with different stuff. Ended up with Boingfwip, because reasons..
